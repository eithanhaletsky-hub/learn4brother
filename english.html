📚 דקדוק – Grammar

הדקדוק הוא חוקי השפה – בלי חוקים אי אפשר לבנות משפטים נכונים!

🔵 שמות עצם – Nouns

dog 🐕 | cat 🐈 | house 🏠 | school 🏫 | book 📚 | child 👦
רבים: cat→cats | box→boxes | baby→babies | man→men | child→children

🟢 פעלים – Verbs

run | eat | sleep | play | read | write | swim | jump | fly | sing
הווה he/she/it: run→runs | go→goes | study→studies

🟡 שמות תואר – Adjectives

big / small | happy / sad | fast / slow | hot / cold | new / old | tall / short
a big dog | a happy girl | a cold day

🔴 מילות יחס – Prepositions

in | on | under | next to | behind | in front of | between | above | below
The cat is on the table. | The ball is under the chair.

🟣 זמנים – Tenses

עבר (Past): I played / I went / She ate
הווה (Present): I play every day / He plays every day
עתיד (Future): I will play / She will go tomorrow

🟤 מילים לא סדירות – Irregular Verbs

go → went | eat → ate | run → ran | see → saw
come → came | take → took | give → gave | have → had

📚 בניית משפטים – Building Sentences

משפט בסיסי = נושא (Subject) + פועל (Verb) + מושא (Object)

The dog eats a bone. | She reads a book. | We play football.

📖 משפטים שימושיים + תרגום

💡 סוגי משפטים

⬛ רגיל: The cat is big.
❓ שאלה: Is the cat big? / Where is the cat?
❗ קריאה: What a big cat! / How beautiful!
